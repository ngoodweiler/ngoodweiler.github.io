Latitude Weather Project

The purpose of this project was to look at a randomized list of cities and compare various weather stats to their respective latitudes to see how they might change. The stats used were Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness, and Wind Speed, collected via the OpenWeatherMap API.

The assembled data was organized and analyzed using Python/Pandas and plots were made with Matplotlib. The data used as well as plots for each of the stats and a comparison are available in the navigation bar or in the Visualizations section to the right.
