Lattitude Weather Project

The purpose of this project was to look at a randomized list of cities and compare various weather stats to their respective latitudes. The stats used were Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness, and Wind Speed. The data used as well as plots for each of the stats and a comparison are available in the navigation bar or to the right.
