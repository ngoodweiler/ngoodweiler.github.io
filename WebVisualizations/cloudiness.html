Cloudiness vs Latitude

Cloudiness (percent cloud cover), taken at the time of data collection (Jan 24, 2020), compared to city latitude. There is no apparent relationship between the two, linearly or otherwise. It is interesting to note that there are quite a few datapoints at either 0% or 100% relative to the total number of data points.
